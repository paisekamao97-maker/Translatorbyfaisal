How does free work? ZabānLink uses the Groq API under the hood. You create your own free Groq account and get an API key — Groq gives you generous free usage (millions of tokens per day). Questions
answered

Do I need to pay anything?
No. ZabānLink is completely free. The Groq API also has a generous free tier — no credit card needed. You will never receive a bill from us.
What if I hit Groq's free limit?
Groq's free tier is very generous — most users will never hit it in normal use. If you do, you'll see a "rate limit" message. You can either wait (limits reset daily) or add a small amount of credit to your Groq account.

Is my data private?
Completely. ZabānLink has no backend — your API key lives in your browser only. Voice audio goes directly to Groq's servers (under your own account). We see nothing. Your conversations are never stored.
Does it work offline?
The current free version requires an internet connection to call the Groq API. Offline mode (with a downloaded model) is planned for the upcoming Pro tier.
Can I add it to my home screen?
Yes! On iPhone: Safari → Share (↑) → "Add to Home Screen". On Android: Chrome menu → "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App". It works like a native app.
